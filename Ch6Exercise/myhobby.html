Concerts Yay!

Why I love going to concerts

I love going to concerts because I get to experience the music I love live. I get to see my favorite artists and get to hear their voices live. Going to concerts makes me feel free and happy. I can not imagine not going to a concert ever again, there is something very different about hearing the music you usually listen to on your phone to listening to it live. I would recommend everyone to go to a concert at least once in their life, it is truly life-changing.
